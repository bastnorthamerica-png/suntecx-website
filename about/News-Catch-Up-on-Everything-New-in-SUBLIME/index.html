Catch Up on Everything New in SUBLIME: New Products, Bold Vision
July 04, 2025
This spring, we introduced SUBLIME. Since then, a lot has happened. We've made great progress in product development, in our retail spaces, in sales tools, and in real-world projects. This July, we made everything one step further.
Lineup Product Released
With the product, we have delivered a full lineup of Classic, Standard, Pro, and Max series. You can define everything: size, material, color, and even the layout. Here, the function meets design in a way we've never done before.
Store Upgraded
To match SUBLIME's premium positioning, we've upgraded stores in cities like Shanghai and Xi'an. This July, our Jiaxing flagship will open with the SUBLIME SI system. And with showrooms coming to Bangkok, Barcelona, and Dubai, SUBLIME is going global.
SUBLIME in Remarkable Spaces
In just three months, SUBLIME has appeared in iconic locations — the History Museum at Fudan University and the VIP Center at Hangzhou Tower. And this is just the beginning.
SUBLIME Air: Ultra-Thin, Quietly Powerful
SUBLIME Air is just 3.7mm thin — sleek, strong, and built to last. With AG glass, crisp yet quiet button feedback, matching accessories, and built-in safety, it's a bold new choice for those who want ultra-thin design without compromise.
SUBLIME Ultra: All-in-One, All Yours
SUBLIME Ultra is our most powerful smart panel yet — combining a 12.3-inch touchscreen, customizable button text, built-in smart station, and multi-protocol support. It's the all-in-one hub for high-end, whole-home control.
SUBLIME OS: Minimal Design, Maximum Clarity
SUBLIME OS is a minimal, intuitive system built for Ultra. With a clean black-and-white design, smart room visuals, whole-home status at a glance, and features like Large Font mode, it brings quiet elegance and effortless control to every home.
Fusion Link: Wired and Wireless, Work Together
Fusion Link™ is LifeSmart's unified system that combines the flexibility of wireless with the stability of wired. It merges CoSS® and CoTP into one seamless architecture, making smart home installation easier, faster, and more reliable for new builds and renovations alike.
Elite Collection: Where Metal Meets Simplicity
The Elite Collection is simple and refined, made from anodized aluminum with a soft matte finish. Available in Dune Gold, Dawn Gold, Imperial Gold, Moonlight Silver, Obsidian Black, Porcelain White, and Carbon Gray, its clean lines and smooth texture bring a quiet, understated luxury to modern, minimalist spaces.
Premium Collection: Lights and Shadows
The Premium Collection features six distinct surface patterns — Landscape, Shining, Hammered, Brush, Silk, and Feather — created with fine laser engraving. Each texture offers a different look and feel, making it easy to match with interiors that value subtle detail and a refined finish.
Signature Collection: It's Rare
The Signature Collection is SUBLIME's highest level of craftsmanship, featuring Line and Oil Painting. Using CNC carving, multi-axis engraving, and hand-finishing, each panel shows rich texture and depth. Bold and full of character, it's made for spaces that value individuality and cultural expression.
More Than a Product
SUBLIME is not just about products — it's about the full experience. We've created a high-end aluminum display box for MAX, PRO, Standard, and Classic panels, fully customizable for local needs. For Premium and Signature series, a leather delivery box with gloves and a card adds a personal, memorable touch to every handover.
The Journey of SUBLIME Continues
Over the next three months, you'll find SUBLIME in more LifeSmart stores. We want more people to experience what SUBLIME really brings. So, we've lined up plenty of events for everyone to check out closely.
The grand tour of SUBLIME is just getting started. We'll keep refining every detail and bringing inspiring products along with exciting experiences to our users. Thank you all for your ongoing support and attention. See you next time.
