OVER 12 YEARS
Striving for a Perfect Harmony Between Form and Function

2013

LifeSmart was founded in Hangzhou, our team believe that IoT will be the next big thing after the mobile internet revolution, and set smart home as the first step of our journey.

2014

We launched our 1st gen products, including the smart station, sensors, smart switches and wireless cameras, and got the angel investment from TISIWI capital and one of the Alibaba 18 founders.

2015

We launched BLEND light bulb and strip products, our FRAME camera won the Chicago Good Design Award, and we got the series A investment from SAIF partners.

2016

We launched 2nd gen CUBE & BLEND series, completed first full smart home project (400 apartments, Shanghai), became Alibaba smart office vendor & SONY China smart home strategic partner.

2017

We won the IF Design Award for the new video door lock, integrated with major AI speakers (Amazon, Google, Apple, Alibaba, Baidu, Tencent, Xiaomi) - the most on the market.

2018

Cololight won CES Innovation Award. Partnered with 3 major China telecom operators for large-scale product delivery. Completed volume delivery for multiple smart residential projects.

2019

We launched the Nature pad, made volume delivery in Japan and Germany, which have been known as the most quality sensitive market, and became SoftBank IoT partners.

2020

Nature Series won the IF Design Award, Red Dot Design Award and Good Design Award. Also we have achieved large-scale implementation and delivery of multiple real estate projects in 2020.

2021

We became the designated smart home supplier for the Beijing Winter Olympic Village, successfully delivering a full system to showcase Chinese technology.

2022

We launched Nature X, a desktop smartwatch that revolutionized the market. It was the first device of its kind to offer powerful IoT connectivity across various brands.

2023

We achieved significant success with batch deliveries of our smart home solutions in the UAE, collaborating with prominent real estate developers Binghatti and Arada.

2024

We marked significant milestones in our global expansion journey by opening new offices in both Tokyo and Dubai. We firmly believe that successful globalization is deeply rooted in effective localization.
